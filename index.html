Privacy Policy for EduKids+

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how EduKids+ ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information, including personal information, in connection with your access to and use of our mobile application (the "App").

EduKids+ is an educational application designed for children. We take your privacy and the privacy of children seriously. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD).

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide to Us

When you create an account or use certain features, we may collect:

  • Email Address: Used for authentication, account recovery, and syncing progress across devices. (Via Supabase)
  • User Name / Nickname: Used to personalize the experience within the App.
  • Age/Grade Level: Used to tailor the educational content to the child's level.

B. Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we and our third-party service providers may automatically collect certain information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, such as lessons completed, quizzes taken, and scores achieved. This helps us improve the educational content.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including model, operating system, and general location (country/region) based on IP address, solely for analytics and performance improvements.
  • Advertising IDs: We use AdMob to show age-appropriate advertisements. AdMob may collect the Google Advertising ID (AAID) to serve ads, limit frequency, and detect fraud. We enforce "Child-Directed Treatment" (COPPA) settings on all ad requests.

2. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we verify that the changes affect children's data in a material way, we will provide checking the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
